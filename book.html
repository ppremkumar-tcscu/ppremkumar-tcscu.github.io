This web page is about reading.

I love to read. Reading has always captivated me. If you read you get transported to different worlds.

I like to got to the library. There are a lot of cool books there. Plus there are videogames.

I like to read fiction a bit more than nonfiction. In fiction I like sci-fi, adventure and realistic fiction. In non-fiction I like wars, machines, science and histories about countries.

My favorite author is Rick Riordan and my favorite book from him is either the House of Hades or the Blood of Olympus.
